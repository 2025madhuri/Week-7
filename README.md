# AI Adoption Opportunity Project: FinTech Fraud Detection

## Project Overview

This project evaluates whether a medium-sized FinTech company should invest in AI-powered fraud detection. The objective is to identify evidence-based AI adoption opportunities, assess implementation risks, estimate business value, and provide a practical recommendation for AI adoption.

The project combines market research, business analysis, Power BI dashboarding, cost estimation, and implementation planning to support executive decision-making.

---

## Company Profile

**Company Name:** FinPay Solutions (Hypothetical)

**Industry:** Financial Technology (FinTech)

**Company Size:** Medium-Sized Enterprise (50–200 Employees)

### Services

- Digital Payment Processing
- Merchant Transaction Services
- Online Financial Services
- Fraud Monitoring and Prevention

---

## Business Challenge

As transaction volumes continue to grow, FinPay Solutions faces increasing fraud risks.

Current challenges include:

- High number of false-positive fraud alerts
- Manual transaction review processes
- Increasing fraud-related losses
- Limited scalability of rule-based fraud detection
- Growing operational costs

The company is exploring whether AI can improve fraud detection performance while delivering measurable business value.

---

## Selected AI Use Case

### AI-Powered Fraud Detection

The selected use case focuses on applying AI and machine learning techniques to identify suspicious transactions and reduce fraud-related losses.

### Business Objectives

- Reduce fraud losses
- Improve fraud detection accuracy
- Reduce manual review effort
- Improve customer trust
- Increase operational efficiency

## Key Findings

### Transaction Analysis

- Total Transactions Analyzed: 284,807
- Fraud Transactions: 492
- Fraud Rate: 0.17%

### Business Insights

- Fraudulent transactions represent a very small percentage of total transactions.
- Fraudulent transactions have a significantly higher average transaction value.
- Manual fraud detection becomes increasingly difficult as transaction volumes grow.
- AI-powered fraud detection can improve detection speed and operational efficiency.
