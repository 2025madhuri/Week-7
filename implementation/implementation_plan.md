# Implementation Plan

## Phase 1: Discovery & Validation

Duration: 2 Weeks

Activities:

- Identify key stakeholders
- Review customer support processes
- Analyze support ticket categories
- Define success criteria

Deliverables:

- Business requirements
- Success metrics
- Pilot scope definition

---

## Phase 2: Data Preparation

Duration: 2 Weeks

Activities:

- Collect historical support tickets
- Clean and categorize data
- Identify common customer inquiries
- Define knowledge base content

Deliverables:

- Prepared training data
- Knowledge repository

---

## Phase 3: Prototype Development

Duration: 4 Weeks

Activities:

- Configure AI model
- Develop chatbot interface
- Integrate support workflows
- Internal testing

Deliverables:

- Working prototype
- Initial performance report

---

## Phase 4: Pilot Program

Duration: 6 Weeks

Activities:

- Deploy to selected customer segment
- Monitor AI responses
- Gather customer feedback
- Measure performance metrics

Success Criteria:

- 30% reduction in repetitive support tickets
- Improved response times
- Positive customer feedback

Deliverables:

- Pilot evaluation report
- ROI assessment

---

## Phase 5: Rollout & Training

Duration: 4 Weeks

Activities:

- Expand deployment
- Train support teams
- Establish governance processes
- Finalize operational procedures

Deliverables:

- Production deployment
- User training materials

---

## Phase 6: Monitoring & Continuous Improvement

Ongoing

Activities:

- Monitor performance metrics
- Review AI accuracy
- Update knowledge base
- Implement improvements

Key KPIs:

- Customer Satisfaction Score
- Average Response Time
- Ticket Deflection Rate
- Operational Cost Savings
