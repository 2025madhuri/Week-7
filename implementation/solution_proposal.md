# Solution Proposal

## Project Overview

Cleo is a mid-sized FinTech company exploring opportunities to improve operational efficiency and customer experience through Artificial Intelligence.

After evaluating several AI adoption opportunities, the recommended use case is implementing an AI-Powered Customer Support Assistant.

---

## Business Problem

As Cleo grows, customer support teams face increasing volumes of inquiries related to account management, transactions, onboarding, and general product questions.

Current challenges include:

- High support workload
- Increased response times
- Rising operational costs
- Inconsistent customer experiences

These challenges impact customer satisfaction and scalability.

---

## Recommended Solution

Implement an AI-powered Customer Support Assistant capable of:

- Answering frequently asked questions
- Providing instant responses 24/7
- Assisting with account-related inquiries
- Routing complex issues to human agents
- Supporting customer onboarding

The solution combines AI-powered natural language processing with existing support workflows.

---

## AI Components

### AI-Driven Features

- Natural language understanding
- Automated response generation
- Intent detection
- Customer query classification

### Standard Automation Components

- Ticket creation
- Escalation workflows
- CRM integration
- Reporting and analytics

---

## Expected Benefits

### Operational Benefits

- Reduced support workload
- Faster response times
- Improved scalability

### Customer Benefits

- 24/7 availability
- Faster issue resolution
- Improved customer experience

### Financial Benefits

- Reduced support costs
- Increased operational efficiency
- Better resource allocation

---

## Recommendation

Recommendation: Run a Small Pilot

A pilot approach minimizes risk while allowing Cleo to validate business value before committing to a full-scale implementation.

The pilot should focus on handling common customer inquiries and measuring key performance indicators such as response time, ticket volume reduction, and customer satisfaction.
